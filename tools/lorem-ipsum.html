Lorem Ipsum Generator

Generate placeholder text for your mockups, websites, and designs.

About this Tool

The Lorem Ipsum Generator creates standard, readable dummy text for designers, developers, and writers. It helps you visualize how text will look on a web page or in a layout without being distracted by the actual content.

How to Use

  1. Enter the number of paragraphs you need in the input field.
  2. Select whether you want plain text or HTML formatted text (with paragraph tags).
  3. Click the "Generate" button.
  4. Review the generated text and click "Copy" to save it to your clipboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Lorem Ipsum mean?

Lorem Ipsum is scrambled, nonsensical Latin text derived from Cicero's 1st-century BC work "De finibus bonorum et malorum." It has been used by printers and typesetters since the 1500s.

Why use dummy text instead of real text?

Dummy text keeps reviewers and designers focused on the layout, typography, and visual hierarchy, rather than getting distracted by reading the content.

Is there a limit to how much text I can generate?

This tool allows you to generate up to 100 paragraphs at once, ensuring your browser remains fast and responsive.
