About this Tool

The Word Counter provides real-time statistics on your text, including word count, character count, and sentence count. It also estimates the reading time of your content. This tool is perfect for writers, students, and content creators who need to meet strict length requirements or optimize their content for readability.

How to Use

  1. Start typing or paste your document directly into the large text area.
  2. Look above the text box to see the stats update in real-time.
  3. Review your total word count, character count, and sentence count.
  4. Check the estimated reading time to see how long it takes an average person to read your text.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the reading time calculated?

The estimated reading time is based on an average adult reading speed of about 200 to 250 words per minute.

Does the character count include spaces?

Yes, the standard character count includes spaces, punctuation, and all symbols, exactly as word processors like Microsoft Word calculate it.

Will my text be saved if I accidentally close the tab?

Currently, the word counter does not automatically save your text to local storage. Be sure to copy your work before closing the window.
