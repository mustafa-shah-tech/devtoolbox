About this Tool

The Percentage Calculator is a handy utility for quick math problems involving percentages. Whether you need to find the percentage of a specific number, calculate percentage increases or decreases for sales and discounts, or determine the ratio between two values, this tool provides instant and accurate results.

How to Use

  1. Choose the type of percentage calculation you need from the three available options.
  2. Enter your specific numbers into the "X" and "Y" input fields.
  3. Click the "Calculate" button next to your chosen row.
  4. The final percentage or numerical result will instantly display on the right.

Frequently Asked Questions

What formulas does the calculator use?

It uses standard mathematical formulas: (X/100)*Y for finding a percentage, (X/Y)*100 for finding ratios, and ((Y-X)/X)*100 for calculating percentage change.

Can I calculate a percentage decrease?

Yes. By using the third row ("% increase/decrease from X to Y") and entering a lower number for Y than X, it will calculate the exact percentage decrease.

Are negative numbers supported?

Yes, you can input negative numbers, and the calculator will correctly apply standard mathematical rules to determine the result.
