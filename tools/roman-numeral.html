Roman Numeral Converter

Convert numbers to Roman numerals and back instantly.

About this Tool

The Roman Numeral Converter easily translates modern numbers (Arabic numerals) into classic Roman numerals, and vice versa. It's a great utility for history students, crossword puzzle solvers, and developers working with specialized formatting requirements.

How to Use

  1. Enter either a standard number (like 42) or a Roman numeral (like XLII) into the input box.
  2. Click the "Convert" button.
  3. The converted value will instantly appear in the result box.
  4. Click "Copy" to save the result to your clipboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the highest number I can convert?

Standard Roman numerals technically only go up to 3,999 (MMMCMXCIX). This tool supports valid conversions up to that limit.

Can I convert Roman numerals to regular numbers?

Yes! The tool automatically detects if you've entered a Roman numeral (using letters like I, V, X, L, C, D, M) and will convert it back to a standard number.

Are lowercase letters accepted?

Yes, you can input Roman numerals in either uppercase or lowercase letters. The converter handles them perfectly.
