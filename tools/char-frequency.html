About this Tool

The Character Frequency Counter analyzes any block of text and calculates exactly how many times each letter, number, or symbol appears. It is highly useful for cryptography (like solving substitution ciphers), data analysis, and checking keyboard layout efficiencies.

How to Use

  1. Paste your text into the large input box.
  2. Toggle the "Ignore Case" or "Ignore Spaces" checkboxes based on your needs.
  3. Click the "Count Frequencies" button.
  4. Review the generated table to see the exact count and percentage bar for each character, sorted from most to least frequent.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does 'Ignore Case' mean?

When 'Ignore Case' is checked, the tool treats uppercase (A) and lowercase (a) letters as the exact same character, combining their counts.

Is there a limit to how much text I can analyze?

The tool can easily handle millions of characters directly in your browser without sending any data to a server.

Are emojis supported?

Yes, standard emojis and symbols are fully supported and will appear in the frequency list alongside standard text.
