About this Tool

The Age Calculator is a simple, precise tool that helps you determine your exact age in years, months, and days from your date of birth. It's especially useful for filling out official forms, calculating age differences, or finding out exactly how old you'll be on a specific future date.

How to Use

  1. Select your date of birth using the calendar input field.
  2. (Optional) Change the "Calculate age at this date" field to a past or future date if you don't want to use today's date.
  3. Click the "Calculate Age" button.
  4. View your exact age broken down by years, months, and days.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is my age calculated?

The calculator finds the exact difference between your birth date and the target date, accounting for leap years and the varying lengths of months.

Is my birth date stored anywhere?

No, this tool operates 100% in your browser. Your birth date is never sent to a server or stored.

Can I calculate age for a future date?

Yes, simply change the target date field to a future date to see exactly how old you will be at that time.
